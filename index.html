How to Play

  • 1
    Click on any card to flip it and reveal the hidden symbol
  • 2
    Click on a second card to try to find its matching pair
  • 3
    If the cards match, they stay flipped. If not, they turn back over
  • 4
    Remember where the symbols are and try to match all cards with minimal moves
  • 5
    Complete the game to see your performance stats and rating
